About Us

Yiwu Shuihui Import & Export Co., Ltd. (also known as Shuihui Hardware) is a professional door hardware manufacturer and exporter based in Wenzhou, China - the hardware capital of the world. With over 10 years of experience, we specialize in supplying high-quality building hardware to the African market, particularly Nigeria.

Our product range includes door handles, door hinges, door locks, sliding door tracks, cabinet hardware, and complete building hardware solutions for construction projects. We serve wholesalers, retailers, and distributors across Africa with factory-direct prices and reliable quality.

Why Choose Us?

  • 10+ years experience in hardware manufacturing and export
  • Factory-direct prices - 30-50% lower than local suppliers
  • Strict quality control - ISO 9001 certified manufacturing
  • Fast delivery - 15-25 days to Lagos, Nigeria
  • OEM/ODM services - Custom branding and packaging
  • English-speaking team - Professional communication
  • Small MOQ - Flexible for wholesalers and retailers

Our Markets

We primarily serve the African market, with a focus on Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa. Our products are designed to meet the quality standards and price requirements of these markets. We understand the unique needs of African importers and work closely with our partners to ensure successful business relationships.

Quality Assurance

Quality is our top priority. We have strict quality control processes in place:

  • Raw material inspection before production
  • In-process quality checks during manufacturing
  • Final inspection before packaging
  • Pre-shipment inspection for large orders
  • Third-party inspection available upon request

Our Mission

To become the most trusted door hardware supplier for African importers by providing high-quality products at competitive prices with reliable service. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our customers and supporting their business growth.

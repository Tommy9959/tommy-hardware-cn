About Us

Yiwu Shuihui Import & Export Co., Ltd. (also known as Tommy Hardware) is a professional door hardware manufacturer and exporter based in Wenzhou, China - the hardware capital of the world. With over 10 years of experience, we specialize in supplying high-quality building hardware to the African market, particularly Nigeria.


Our product range includes door handles, door hinges, door locks, sliding door tracks, cabinet hardware, and complete building hardware solutions for construction projects. We serve wholesalers, retailers, and distributors across Africa with factory-direct prices and reliable quality.

❓ How long does shipping to Nigeria take?

Shipping to Lagos, Nigeria typically takes 15-25 days by sea. We work with reliable shipping agents to ensure safe delivery to Lagos port. We can also arrange air freight for urgent orders (3-7 days).

❓ Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Yes, we offer both OEM and ODM services. We can customize products with your brand logo, packaging design, and specifications. Our design team can also develop new products based on your market requirements.

❓ What are your payment terms?

We accept multiple secure payment methods:

  • T/T (Telegraphic Transfer): Bank-to-bank wire transfer - most common for large orders
  • L/C at Sight: Letter of Credit with bank guarantee - recommended for orders >$10,000
  • XTransfer: Fast online payment - ideal for samples and small orders

Standard Terms:

  • New customers: 30% deposit, 70% before shipment
  • Regular customers: Flexible terms based on order history
  • Samples: 100% advance payment

For Nigeria market, we recommend T/T or confirmed L/C for secure transactions.

❓ How do you ensure product quality?

We have strict quality control: 1) Raw material inspection, 2) In-process checks, 3) Final inspection, 4) Pre-shipment inspection. We provide quality certificates and can arrange third-party inspection.

❓ Can I get samples before placing an order?

Yes, we provide free samples for quality evaluation. Customers only need to pay for shipping. Sample delivery takes 3-7 days by express courier.

❓ What markets do you serve?

We primarily serve the African market, with a focus on Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa. Our products are designed to meet the quality standards and price requirements of these markets.
